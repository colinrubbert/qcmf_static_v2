About Us

Quality Custom Metal Fabrication since 1992, has turned customer ideas into functional realities through cutting edge metal fabrication technology and unsurpassed customer service.

In February of 1992 QCMF opened its doors in a 2000 square foot building and focused mainly on guardwork. Currently we have 20,000 square feet where we have an indoor inventory of cold rolled, galvanized, stainless and aluminum sheet usually ranging from 10 – 20 GA, plate from 3/16” to 1”, and various sizes of square and round tube as well as flat bar and round stock in Hot rolled, cold rolled, stainless, and aluminum.

Serving the Fox Valley area industries and businesses for years with expert, custom metal fabricators, we at QCMF have gained an excellent reputation for craftsmanship. Our experienced staff takes pride in their work, producing quality products with outstanding service and on time delivery as well as quick turn around.

Based in Neenah, WI, we feature the latest in technology, have the ability to handle most any project, specialize in customized metal enclosures and provide machine guard work. We employ experienced professionals trained in the science of metal fabrication who are always ready to serve you in a friendly, efficient manner. Call us today at (920) 727-4540. We are dedicated to meeting your needs.
